Fun facts about misunderstood animals

Learn what makes each species important to ecosystems, then test your knowledge with quick activities. Same color vibe, just a cleaner and calmer look.

Bats

Bats are major pollinators and natural pest control helpers. One bat can eat thousands of insects in a night.

Opossums

Opossums eat ticks, insects, and garden pests. They are gentle scavengers that help keep neighborhoods cleaner.

Vultures

Vultures reduce disease spread by cleaning up carrion quickly. They play a key role in healthy ecosystems.
